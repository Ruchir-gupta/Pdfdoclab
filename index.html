Why use browser-based tools?

  • ✅ Instant: No waiting for uploads to finish.
  • ✅ Private: Your sensitive documents never leave your RAM.
  • ✅ Free: We don't have server costs, so you don't pay.

About PDFDocLab

How it works?

PDFDocLab uses Client-Side Processing. When you "upload" a file, it is loaded into your browser's local memory. The JavaScript code then manipulates the data and triggers a download. 0% of your data is sent to any server.

Privacy Policy

We do not use databases. We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not see your files. This is a strictly "What You See Is What You Get" tool.

Usage Limits & Safety

  • Text: Recommended up to 50,000 words.
  • Images: Up to 20MB per image for stable results.
  • PDF Merge: Recommended for files under 25MB total.
